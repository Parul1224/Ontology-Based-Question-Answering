



  Cercospora Leaf Spot of Cotton 

   Mycosphaerella gossypina 





फफूंद





संक्षेप में





                Initially, reddish lesions on leaves, later enlarging and turning grayish to brown with a purple or dark margin.

              

                As the spots enlarge, their margins show an alternation of light and dark concentric rings.
