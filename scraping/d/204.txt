

            





  Bacterial Spot of Citrus 

   Xanthomonas alfalfae 





बैक्टीरिया





संक्षेप में





                Primarily a disease of trifoliate orange and its hybrids, e.g.

              

                Swingle citrumelo in nursery conditions.

              

                Flat or sunken, roundish lesions with brown necrotic centers that often drop out, leaving a "shot-hole".

              

                Water-soaked margins and diffuse yellow halo.

              

                Severely infected leaves become chlorotic or scorched and may drop early.
